COOKIE POLICY

Last updated November 24, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Moms Connect ("Company," "we," "us," and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://new-city-connect.replit.app (the "Website"). It also explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and the choices you have to control their use.

In some cases, we may use cookies to collect personal information, or information that becomes personal information if we combine it with other details.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, to work more efficiently, and to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, Moms Connect) are called first-party cookies. Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called third-party cookies. Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (for example, analytics and performance tools). The parties that set these cookies can recognize your device when it visits our Website and when it visits certain other websites.

Why do we use cookies?

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons:

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our Website to operate. They enable core functionality such as user login, session management, and security. Without these cookies, services you have asked for cannot be provided.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: These cookies help us understand how the Website is being used, such as which pages are visited most often and any errors users might encounter. This information is used to improve how our Website works and how we deliver our services.

Third parties may serve cookies through our Website for analytics and performance purposes only. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

How can I control cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Because essential cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with our services, they cannot be disabled. For non-essential cookies, you can control and manage them through your browser settings.

If you choose to block or delete certain cookies, some features of the Website may not function properly or be available.

How to manage cookies in your browser

The way you can manage cookies depends on the browser you are using. For more information, you can visit the help pages for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Microsoft Edge / Internet Explorer
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Opera

Other tracking technologies

Cookies are not the only way to recognize or track visitors. We may use similar technologies from time to time, such as web beacons (also known as clear gifs or tracking pixels). These are tiny graphics files that contain a unique identifier that enables us to recognize when someone has visited our Website or opened an email that we have sent.

These technologies help us monitor traffic patterns, measure the success of certain features, and improve overall performance. Many of these technologies rely on cookies, so disabling cookies may impair their function.

Do you use Flash cookies or Local Shared Objects?

We do not intentionally use Flash cookies (also known as Local Shared Objects or "LSOs") on our Website. However, older browser plugins or content on other websites you visit may use them.

If you want to limit or disable Flash cookies, you can adjust your Flash Player settings using Adobe's tools. Please note that limiting Flash cookies may reduce the functionality of some Flash-based content.

Do you serve targeted advertising?

No. We do not use targeted advertising, interest-based advertising, or retargeting technologies on our Website. No third-party advertisers deliver ads through our Website or App. Any third-party cookies used in connection with our Website are limited to analytics, performance, and basic site functionality.

How often will you update this Cookie Policy?

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this Cookie Policy indicates when it was most recently revised.
